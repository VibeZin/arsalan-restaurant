# Media Assets

Place your restaurant photos here. The website uses these files:

## `/media/hero/`
- `hero-bg.jpg` — Hero section background (recommended: 1920×1080px)
  - A dark, atmospheric photo of the restaurant, grill, or food
  - The image is rendered at 40% opacity over a dark overlay

## `/media/dishes/`
- `seekh-kebab.jpg` — Seekh Kebab signature dish card
- `nihari.jpg` — Beef Nihari signature dish card
- `butter-naan.jpg` — Butter Naan signature dish card
- `chapli-kebab.jpg` — Chapli Kebab signature dish card
  - Recommended size: 800×600px (4:3 ratio)

## `/media/gallery/`
- `gallery-01.jpg` through `gallery-08.jpg` — Gallery grid images
  - gallery-01 and gallery-06 span 2 columns (use square/portrait photos)
  - Remaining 6 are standard 4:3 landscape

## `/media/logo/`
- `logo-white.png` — Restaurant logo (white on transparent, ~80×80px)

---

**Tip:** All placeholders are gracefully shown if images are missing,
so the site works without any images too.
